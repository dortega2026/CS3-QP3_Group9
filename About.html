Here is everything you need to know about our mini games!

Hampasin Mo 'Ko

Hampasin Mo 'Ko is basically just another version of Whack-a-Mole, but instead of using the usual characters for this game, we used JYP and Sana to make the game more fun. I know it seems a little unusual because of our character choice but trust me on this when I say that it's fun and enjoyable to play.

To Play This Game:
1. In this game, Hampasin Mo 'Ko, your main goal is to avoid Sana & whack the mole which is JYP.

2. The game won't end as long you click JYP so you can aim for the highest score you can possibly imagine.

3. The score increases in increments of 10 and the game will only end if you click on Sana.

Source Code/Reference: https://www.youtube.com/live/z0cKwzPF9cQ?feature=share

Maalaala Mo Kaya

The game Maalaala Mo Kaya is basically a memory game. In this game, you need to match all the cards. This game is perfect for people who want to build their thinking skills, concentration, attention, persistence, and improve other brain functions, such as focus.

To Play This Game:
1. The game starts with all the cards face down and the players should turn over two cards.

2. If the two cards have the same picture, then the cards will stay face up.

3. If the two cards don't have the same picture, then the cards will turn face down again.

4. Finally, the player will win once the cards are all matched and facing up.

Source Code/Reference: https://github.com/kaizhelam/Memory-Game and https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mohIQB_70Xk

3072 (2048 pero mas maganda)

Just like what the game title suggests, this game is basically just 2048 but better. How so? Because we said so duhhh. It is a fun and easy puzzle that requires little to no luck at all. All it takes is a simple strategy to solve it. You don't need to love numbers just to love this game. It doesn't even require knowledge in Math!

To Play This Game:
1.) 2 tiles that slide left to right and up to down containing the number three will be seen at the start of the game; the arrow keys will be used to move them.

2.) New tiles with the number three will appear on a random empty tile.

3.) The player should preferably reach the number 3072 or higher.

4.) The player will lose if there are no possible moves, and they can click the restart button to restart the game.

Source Code/Reference: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JSn-DJU8qf0&t=0s
